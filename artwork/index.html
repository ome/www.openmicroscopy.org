Feel free to:

  • Use the OME logo or mark to link to our website
  • Use the mark in social buttons to link to our website
  • Change the dimension so long as you maintain the aspect ratio

Please DON’T:

  • Integrate the OME logo or mark into your logo (e.g. placing your logo "inside" of ours)
  • Use any of our logos for your application’s icon
  • Create a modified version of any of our logos
  • Change the colors of our logos
  • Change the dimensions or add your own text/images
