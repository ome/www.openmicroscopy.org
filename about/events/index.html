12th Annual Users Meeting 2017

The 12th Annual OME Users Meeting was held at the University of Dundee on May 31st to June 2nd 2017.

We are in the process of moving content to this new site at present. Details of earlier meetings are still available at [https://www.openmicroscopy.org/site/community/minutes/meetings]
