Organisers: ELMI 2025

Location: Heidelberg, Germany

Workshop: Imaging workflows in OMERO

Time and Date: 4 June 2025, at 14.30

Duration: 1 hour

This workshop covers all of the main functions of OMERO. We explain the import to OMERO and then demonstrated organisation, viewing, searching, annotation and publishing of images using OMERO. After we cover the basics of OMERO, we shortly explain the principles of how 3rd party image analysis packages work with OMERO. This enables the participants to understand the manual data processing and automated processing workflows using a range of open source applications running alongside OMERO, such as ImageJ/Fiji or CellPose.
