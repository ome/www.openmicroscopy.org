Organisers: LCI at Karolinska Institutet

Location: Stockholm, OMERO and OMERO.figure workshop is held remotely

Date: 15th February 2022

Duration: 2 hours

This online course focuses on explanation of basics of OMERO and OMERO.figure. Both parts will give encouragement for hands-on work with concrete examples and tasks. The main theme of the workshop are the publication possibilities in OMERO and OMERO.figure.
