Day 2 — Wednesday 3rd June, 2015

The plan for the workshops this year features a mixture of presentations and more informal discussion topics. Please feel free to approach a member of the team with your questions or to request a demonstration of the OMERO clients or specific functionality. If you need to leave early and want a specific topic covered earlier than it is listed in the programme, please let the workshop leaders know as early as possible and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

09:00 - 10:30

Workshops - Round 1

  • Room 1 - Working with OMERO
  • Informal demonstrations of OMERO.insight and OMERO.web
  • Informal demonstrations of other tools developed by the OME consortium e.g. FLIMfit, ImageJ
  • Discussions as requested on integrating your own tools: Java, Python etc.
  • Discussions as requested on using/customizing training materials
  • Room 2 - Managing OMERO
  • Presentation on advanced import features: in-place, dropbox, import-for etc.
  • Public Repository: permissions, deployment & customization
  • Room 3 - Working with Bio-Formats (developer-aimed session)
  • Introduction to working with Bio-Formats
  • Presentation on working with Bio-Formats in C++
  • Breakout sessions on working with Bio-Formats in Java & Matlab and with large datasets
  • Followed by discussion on HDF5, Bio-Formats and OMERO
