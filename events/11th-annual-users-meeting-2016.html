Day 2 — Wednesday 1st June, 2016 - Dalhousie Teaching Rooms

The workshops will feature a mixture of presentations and more informal discussion topics. Please feel free to approach a member of the team with your questions or to request a demonstration of the OMERO clients or specific functionality.

09:15 - 10:30

Workshops - Round 1

  • Room 1 - Working with OMERO/OMERO for Users
  • Informal demonstrations of OMERO.insight and OMERO.web
  • Informal demonstrations of other tools developed by the OME consortium e.g. FLIMfit, ImageJ
  • Discussions as requested on integrating your own tools: Java, Python etc.
  • Discussions as requested on using/customizing training materials
  • Room 2 - @Scale: Technologies for managing OMERO
  • Introduction to OMERO at scale
  • Tools for configuration, testing and deployment management (e.g. Docker) ( Movie version)
  • Image Data Repository (IDR) case study (OpenStack, GPFS, public web, caching) ( Movie version)

  • Introduction to working with Bio-Formats ( Movie version)
  • OME File Formats ( Movie version)
  • Breakout sessions on working with Bio-Formats in Java & MATLAB
  • Working with large datasets
