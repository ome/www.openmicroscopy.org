Organisers: LCI at Karolinska Institutet

Location: Stockholm, OMERO and OMERO.figure workshop was held remotely

Date: 12th February 2025

Duration: 2 hours

This online course focuses on the basics of OMERO and OMERO.figure. Both parts give encouragement for hands-on work with concrete examples and tasks. The main theme of the workshop is the publication possibilities in OMERO and OMERO.figure.
